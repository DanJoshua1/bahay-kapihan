About Us

Welcome to Bahay Kapihan - your cozy coffee destination in Caloocan City

Our Story

Bahay Kapihan started with a simple dream: to create a warm and welcoming space where people can enjoy quality coffee in a homey atmosphere. Located in the heart of Emerald Street, we strive to provide our customers with the perfect blend of comfort and exceptional coffee experiences.

Our coffee shop features a carefully curated menu of coffee beverages, from classic espresso-based drinks to unique specialty creations. We take pride in using quality beans and ingredients to ensure every cup meets our high standards.

Our Space

Located in Block 11 Lot 2 Emerald Street Union Village Subdivision Bagumbong, North Caloocan City Near Come to Jesus Community Church, our cafe offers a cozy and comfortable environment perfect for:

  • Studying and working
  • Casual meetings with friends
  • Small gatherings and celebrations
  • Solo coffee moments
